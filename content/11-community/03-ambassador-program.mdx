The Cardano community is privileged to have committed members of our [Ambassadors Program](https://www.cardano.org/ambassadors/), which was established in 2018, and aims to drive the adoption of [Cardano](https://cardano.org/), promote awareness, and educate the wider community. If you would like to join this program, please send us an [email](mailto:community@cardanofoundation.org).
